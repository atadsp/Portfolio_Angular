Random Character Generator

I'm an avid player of role playing games, but there is the problem of making characters for those games who dont really have a backstory. This generator aims to rectify that situation. It uses a number of criteria and conditionals to generate off a character with a full backstory and family so that players can have something to start with when playing fantasy setting role playing games.
